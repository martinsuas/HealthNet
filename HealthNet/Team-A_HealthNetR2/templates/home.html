What is HealthNet?

HealthNet is an application that enables hospitals in the US to be able to communicate between employees and patients. It provides an easy mechanism for managing employees, gathering statistical data on the inner workings of the hospital, signing up patients, making appointments, and allowing ease of transfer of both patients and their information between hospitals

Info about HealthNet App

HealthNet App is an original application developed by Team_A. It allows users to register, update patient profile, create patient appointment and view patient medical information.
